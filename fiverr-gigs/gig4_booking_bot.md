## Description

Want customers to book appointments directly in Telegram — without phone calls?

I'll build a **fully automated booking bot** tailored to your business.

### How it works:
1. Customer opens the bot
2. Selects a service
3. Picks available date & time
4. Enters phone number
5. Gets confirmation

**You get instantly notified** in Telegram when someone books.
